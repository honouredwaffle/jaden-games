# Jaden Games Update Log

## April 17, 2026

### Sukuna Domain Expansion - Malevolent Shrine
- **Added**: Barrierless domain expansion for Sukuna character
- **Visuals**: 
  - Shrine manifests behind player with 4 mouths (cardinal directions)
  - 8 skulls around the base with glowing red eyes
  - Pulsing red glow effects
  - Dark shrine body with crimson border
- **Mechanics**:
  - 200m sure-hit radius (no barrier)
  - Continuous cleave/dismantle damage (25 damage per tick, 6 ticks per second)
  - Domain follows player movement
  - 30 second duration
- **Effects**:
  - Visual slice effects spawn randomly within domain
  - Curved cleave slashes and straight dismantle cuts
  - Domain text: "伏魔御廚子" / "MALEVOLENT SHRINE"
  - Subtle radius indicator (dashed circle)
- **Activation**: Press 4 or click DOMAIN ability (80 CE cost)
